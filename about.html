About Hassan Moustafa

Two decades of engineering integrity, genuine parts sourcing, and unwavering commitment to the performance and safety of every vehicle that enters our workshop.

Our Story

Built on Engineering Integrity

Hassan Moustafa Automotive was founded in 2006 with a singular mission: to offer vehicle owners in the UAE a genuine alternative to dealership servicing — one built on technical honesty, transparent pricing, and uncompromising parts authenticity.

What began as a specialist garage for German and Japanese marques has grown into one of Dubai's most respected independent workshops, trusted by thousands of vehicle owners for everything from routine maintenance to full engine reconstructions.

Our philosophy has never changed: diagnose accurately, source genuine parts only, deliver on time, and charge fairly. Every vehicle that enters our workshop — whether a daily commuter or a collector's supercar — receives the same meticulous attention to detail.
